Get Foundation

The Foundation Certificate in Higher Education is intended for students who do not meet the admission requirements for degree programs.

The program provides students with the chance to build and improve the necessary knowledge and skills to succeed at the undergraduate level. Students may enter year 1 of a degree program immediately after completing the program.
